RBI/2025-26/51
Low-risk customer KYC updation relaxation
12 June 2025

Low-risk customers identified as per RBI Master Direction on KYC dated Feb 25, 2016 may continue transactions. Regulated entities must ensure KYC updation within one year of this circular or by 30 June 2026, whichever is earlier. Applicable to banks, NBFCs, and all regulated entities under DOR. Non-compliance attracts penalties under Section 47 of the Banking Regulation Act.
