Reference: DOR.AML.REC.31/14.01.001/2025-26
Department: Department of Regulation (DOR)
Date: 12 June 2025
Category: KYC
Applicable Entities: All Regulated Entities

Subject: Updation / periodic updation of KYC - revised instructions for Regulated Entities.

Key Rule Summary:
Regulated Entities must follow updated KYC periodic updation instructions and refresh customer records per revised timelines and controls.
