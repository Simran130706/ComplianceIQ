RBI/2025-26/75
KYC (2nd Amendment) Directions, 2025
14 August 2025

Amendments to Master Direction - Know Your Customer (KYC) Direction, 2016. Strengthens due diligence norms for high-risk customers. Regulated entities must implement enhanced due diligence (EDD) for PEPs (Politically Exposed Persons). Video-based Customer Identification Process (V-CIP) now mandatory for onboarding above ₹10 lakh threshold. All entities must update internal KYC policies within 90 days. Failure to comply will be treated as violation of Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) 2002.
