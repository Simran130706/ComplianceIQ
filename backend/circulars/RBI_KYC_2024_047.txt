RBI Circular RBI/KYC/2024/047 dated February 28, 2024
Subject: Enhanced KYC Requirements for NRI Account Operations

1. All NRI accounts must complete Video KYC re-verification by June 30, 2024.
2. Overseas address proof must be updated every 2 years. Dormant NRI accounts with outdated KYC must be frozen.
3. NRI accounts with transactions above INR 15,00,000 require source-of-funds declaration within 48 hours.
4. PAN-Aadhaar linkage is mandatory for all NRI accounts by March 31, 2024. Non-compliance results in account freeze.
5. FEMA compliance report must be submitted quarterly for NRI accounts exceeding aggregate balance of INR 50,00,000.
